About Me

From hardware to code—a journey of rediscovering my passion for technology.

I'm a web developer based in Orange County, California. My tech journey began with computer electronics at DeVry University, but after losing interest in the hardware side, I thought my future in technology was over. Everything changed when my then-fiancée encouraged me to explore programming after learning about my background and aspirations.

The moment I successfully traced my first "for" loop, I was hooked. That sense of accomplishment sparked a passion that led me to complete an immersive web development bootcamp. Today, I build full-stack web applications from the ground up, specializing in React, JavaScript, and PHP. My goal is to develop innovative mobile solutions that help small businesses connect seamlessly with their clients. When I'm not coding, you'll find me surfing or practicing archery—both pursuits I've taught myself through dedication and persistence.
